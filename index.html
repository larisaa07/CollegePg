Student Life

Popular Clubs:

  • USC Student Government: Involved in campus governance and student advocacy, offering a platform for students to voice their opinions.
  • USC Concerts Committee: Organizes music events and concerts, bringing popular artists to campus.
  • Greek Life: With numerous fraternities and sororities, Greek life fosters community, leadership, and social opportunities.
  • Society of Hispanic Professional Engineers (SHPE): Focuses on promoting the development of Hispanic engineers through professional development and community service.
  • USC Debate Squad: A competitive organization that participates in national debate tournaments, fostering critical thinking and public speaking skills.
  • Black Student Union (BSU): Provides a supportive space for Black students and hosts cultural and educational events.
  • Environmental Student Assembly: Engages students in sustainability efforts and environmental awareness initiatives.
  • USC Film Society: A haven for film enthusiasts, hosting screenings, discussions, and film-related events.

Dorming:

Most dorms are located close to the central campus, making it easy to access classes, libraries, and recreational facilities. Living on campus encourages a strong sense of community, with Resident Advisors (RAs) available to support students and organize activities.

  • Traditional Halls: These usually have shared bathrooms and offer a more communal living experience. They are great for meeting new people and often host social events.
  • Suite-Style Housing: Suites typically include a shared living space and private bathrooms for a smaller group of residents. This setup provides more privacy while still encouraging community.
  • Apartment-Style Housing: Ideal for upperclassmen, these often feature full kitchens and living rooms, giving residents more independence.
  • Theme Housing: Some dorms focus on specific themes, like cultural identities, academic interests, or lifestyle choices, fostering a strong sense of community among like-minded students.
